About us

Vedic Degree College, located near Sambalpur, Odisha, is part of the Vedic Group of Institutions, offering undergraduate courses in Arts, Science, and Commerce (B.A., B.Sc., B.Com), plus BBA & BCA, focusing on holistic development with modern tech like coding alongside traditional values in a green, well-equipped campus. The institution emphasizes quality education, producing achievers, and provides a supportive environment with experienced faculty, hostel facilities, and various extracurriculars like TEDx talks.

Key Features

  • Academics: Offers +3 Science (Physics, Chemistry, Maths, Botany, Zoology), +3 Commerce, +3 Arts (English, Political Science, Odia) and professional courses like BBA & BCA.
  • Environment: Set on a 20-acre campus with modern infrastructure, labs, playgrounds, and a serene atmosphere conducive to learning.
  • Holistic Development: Organizes programs like TEDx Talks and Model UN to build life skills.
  • Technology Integration: Includes Robotics and Coding in its curriculum, following international standards.
  • Faculty: Features well-qualified and passionate educators from across the country.
  • Facilities: Provides well-equipped hostels with AC, medical assistance (doctors/nurses on campus), canteens, and recreational spaces.
  • Recognition: Part of the ISO 9001:2015 certified Vedic Group of Institutions and has received awards like ASSOCHAM EDUSHINE Excellence Award.

Location

Pabpali, Sason, Sambalpur, Odisha.
